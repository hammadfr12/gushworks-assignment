Since 1998

Our Journey

From a 500 sq ft workshop to a 40,000 sq ft state-of-the-art manufacturing facility.

1998
Foundation
Founded in Surat, Gujarat. Started supplying yarn guides and tension devices to local mills.
2004
First Machine
Manufactured our first complete Two For One Twister. Supplied to 8 factories in Gujarat.
2010
ISO Certification
Achieved ISO 9001 certification. Expanded to 15,000 sq ft facility. First export to Bangladesh.
2016
Digital Controls
Launched VFD and PLC-controlled series. Energy consumption reduced by 30%.
2023
Global Reach
Now exporting to 12 countries. 500+ machines operational worldwide. New 40,000 sq ft facility launched.
